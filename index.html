ABOUT YOU

Fella, who's real name is Devonte Edwards, born March 16, 1994 is an upandcoming Artist/Rapper from the city of New Orleans, La. He's been playing with words since the growing age of 8 years old and was only 12 years old when he first started recording music. Being born in the St.Bernard Projects (7th Ward) the year New Orleans had the highest murder rate in the country, while growing up in a generation that was to come led the young artist to go astray and become a product of his very own enviornment, in which he was charged with Armed Robbery in 2009 and tried as an adult at the young age of 15 years old, and was sentenced to 10 years in prison. During the first 14 months of his incarceration Fella's mother Brenda Edwards got very sick and passed away, he was able to attend his mother's funeral as an inmate from Dixon Correctional Institute (D.C.I) while he was handcuffed and shackled from his wrist to his ankles.
